About Me!

Hello there! My name is Jasmine! I'm currently 30 years old and a self-taught digital artist. I've been drawing since I was a little girl & watching anime and playing video games has inspired me to improve my skills. I'm currently a college student obtaining my Associate's Degree in Visual Arts and planning to transfer to Virginia State Univeristy or Virginia Tech to obtain my Bachelor's Degree in 3D Animation. My dream is to make my own 3D animated series in the future!
